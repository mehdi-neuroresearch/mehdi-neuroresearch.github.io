From an early age, I have been deeply curious about the human mind and the mechanisms behind behavior and emotion.

My encounters with anxiety and panic attacks motivated me to study emotional regulation and neural mechanisms behind behavioral change.

My research focuses on narrative identity, addiction recovery and identity-related neural pathways.

Research Philosophy

My research explores how narrative identity, cognitive processes and neural mechanisms interact to shape human behavior and reconstruct identity-related neural pathways.
