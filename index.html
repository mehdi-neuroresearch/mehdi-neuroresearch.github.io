Mehdi Zahmetkesh

Neuroscience Researcher

Independent researcher exploring the intersection of neuroscience, narrative therapy, identity transformation, and behavioral change. My work focuses on understanding how neural processes, personal narratives, and cognitive frameworks shape human behavior and psychological well-being. My research explores innovative approaches to mental health, including addiction recovery, panic attacks, stress regulation, and long-term behavioral transformation. Alongside my research, I also write books and educational materials that translate complex psychological and neuroscientific ideas into practical insights for personal growth and mental well-being.
